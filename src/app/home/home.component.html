NIE

You make moneyby moving inventory, not storing it. Every distributer has a dead-stock graveyard on their property.

When youy inventoy doesn't move for various reasons it adds to your holding cost. It's a cash flow killer. And while inventory management software keeps track of sales and other informations like the date of exiry, allowable return dates, and so on, a certain amount of dead-stock is inevitable despite taking measures. By joining the NIE network you can mitigate the impact of dead-stock in a manner that is separate and complimentary to your inventory management software.

WE OFFER

Distributors wishing to re-market unused products use the NIE platform to post items they wish to offer to fellow members of the NIE...

The NIE application features a negotiation algorithm that protects the identify of the parties untill a buy/sell agreement has been made and completes the transaction with best practice payment and shipping ergonomics.

TO EXCHANGE

Using the exchange platform is as easy as entering data in any GUI database

The data fields guide you through a product posting which then becomes agnostically identified on the respective product indices for buyers to consider. Once a buyer has declared intent, a two-way exchange begins the process of completing a transaction according to the requirements of both parties. Transaction processing and shipping specifies complete the deal and the seller's "SKU" is deleted.
